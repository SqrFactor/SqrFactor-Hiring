Can Individuals also take part in the Competition?

Yes, Individuals, Team of 2 or 3 are eligible to participate in the competition.


How can we form a Team? Is it compulsory to form a team with a student of the same college? Can students team up with a fresher Architect?

No, not necessarily that the students forming a team should belong to the same college. You can team up with a fresher Architect. Also, if you are forming a team with a fresher architect, we would prefer the Architect should register as the Team Member 1.


Is it necessary to have a mentor/teacher guiding the team?

It is not compulsory for a team to have a mentor.


How many number of teams can a Single Teacher/Mentor guide?

There is no limit to the number of teams a single Teacher/Mentor can guide.


I am currently pursuing my post graduation and I have graduated in 2013. Am I also eligible for the competition?

Any Student pursuing B.Arch or M.Arch irresepective of the year of graduation is eligible for the competition. Only Architects, who have graduated before 2014 and who are working as professionals or in an Architecture firm are not eligible to participate in the competition.


Is there an option for paying the registration fee offline?

Yes, you can also pay the fees offline. If you want to pay the fees offline, please register and get the Team Registration code first and send us an email to queries@sqrfactor.in with 'Offline Payment : Team Code' subject. The mail should also contain your Team Details.
