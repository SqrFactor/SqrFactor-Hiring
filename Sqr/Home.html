Envisioning Gwalior Trade Fair

Envisioning Gwalior Trade Fair is a competition ideated and initiated by Gwalior trade fair authority and SqrFactor Design Campaign in association with Indian Institute of Architects and MPCCI.

The competition aims to:

  • To revive Gwalior trade fair and imagining a design solution as a future possibility
  • Bring the architecture fraternity together to deliberate the new emerging ideas and solutions.
  • Promote young and creative minds through larger involvement and participation of the Students.

In Association With

Indian Institute of Architects

Gwalior Trade Fair Authority

Madhya Pradesh Chamber of Commerce

Competition Details

SqrFactor invites all the Architecture students and Young Architects* to send in their entries for our Competition.

  • Team of maximum 3 members
  • Idea based single stage Competition.
  • Special recognition and awards for the Teachers/Mentors* guiding the Team
  • Young Architects*- Fresher Architects graduated in and after 2014 will be eligible for the competition.
